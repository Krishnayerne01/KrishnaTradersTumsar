About Krishna Traders

Your Trusted Furniture Partner Since 2006

Krishna Traders is a well-established furniture shop located at Station Road, Goverdhan Nagar, Tumsar, Maharashtra 441912. Since 2006, we have been dedicated to providing top-quality furniture that brings both style and comfort to your home and office spaces.
We specialize in a wide range of wooden and modern furniture including beds, sofas, dining tables, chairs, wardrobes, and modular solutions. Our mission is to combine durability with beautiful designs that reflect both traditional craftsmanship and contemporary elegance.
At Krishna Traders, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We strive to offer personalized service, honest pricing, and a collection of furniture pieces that truly make your space feel like home.
